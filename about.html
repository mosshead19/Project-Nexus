About Project Nexus

Project Nexus is the software school organization of Palawan State University committed to developing future-ready Computer Science and Information Technology professionals through hands-on training, collaborative projects, and industry-aligned programs.

Bridging Theory and Practice

We bridge the gap between academic learning and real-world practice by conducting technical workshops, seminars, extension projects, and system deployments that immerse students in modern software engineering, deployment strategies, and teamwork environments.

  • Work with real clients, real systems, and real problems
  • Build not only code, but competence, confidence, and leadership
  • Connect theory with application through mentorship and innovation
  • Transform learners into capable developers and technology leaders

Our Mission

To empower Computer Science and Information Technology students of Palawan State University with practical skills, real-world experience, and professional competencies through hands-on training, collaborative projects, and industry-aligned programs that bridge academic learning with professional practice.

Our Vision

To be a recognized student organization within Palawan State University that produces competent software developers and technology professionals equipped with industry-relevant skills and a commitment to serve the technological needs of Palawan and surrounding communities.

What We Offer

Comprehensive programs designed to transform students into industry-ready professionals

Technical Workshops

Hands-on training sessions covering modern technologies, frameworks, and best practices in software development.
